About me

Hello! I'm Malcolm Lismore, a passionate freelance photographer nestled along the captivating North West coast of Scotland. Through my lens, I weave tales of the rugged Scottish landscapes, its diverse wildlife, and the graceful coastal birds. Photography is not just my profession; it's an art form that allows me to freeze moments in time. With a focus on the natural world, I bring a blend of technical expertise and artistic vision to every shot. While my heart lies in capturing the untamed beauty of the Scottish terrain, I also specialize in turning your special occasions into timeless memories, be it weddings, portraits, or events. Join me on this visual journey, and let's create something extraordinary together.
